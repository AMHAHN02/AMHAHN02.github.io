The CardShirt Company

Who We Are

The CardShirt Company is a student run not-for-profit based out of the University of Louisville’s College of Business. Here at CardShirt, we are dedicated to raising funds for student scholarships and providing students with valuable business experience through business partnerships and the design, marketing, and sale of UofL themed spirit wear. The Cardshirt Company works to keep students in college financially and teach them the skills to transition into their post-graduate career.

At CardShirt, our goal is to provide our student staff with invaluable real world experience to prepare themselves for a rewarding career when they graduate college, while also generating scholarships for our peers. We do this by designing, marketing, and selling Cardinal spirit wear and custom apparel for independant clients — all in house and by students.

What We Make

Custom Clothes! We make anything that you as a customer would find appealing! This ranges from U of L Spirit wear, all the way to tees for the Greek Life. We even work with business outside of UofL!
